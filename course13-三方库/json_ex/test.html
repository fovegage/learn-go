Size Chart

Tailored Ultra Wide Leg Pants

Model: 5'11" wearing Size {OFPModelSizeMeasurement}

Our size charts are based on the measurements below. Use a tape measure and these guidelines to determine your size. A friend might come in handy to help you with measuring. If you don’t have a tape measure, you can use a piece of string or ribbon and then measure it with a ruler. Please note: garments may vary due to design and manufacturing differences.

1. BUST

Measure around the fullest part of your bust and keep tape level under your arms and across your back.

2. BRA

Step 1: Band Measurement

Wrap measuring tape just below your bust; the tape should fit snugly.

Step 2: Bust Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your bust. The tape should fit comfortably—not too loose, not too tight. Round to the nearest whole number depending on how you like your fit. Wear a bra with little or no padding when taking this measurement.

Step 3: Bra Size

Centimeters: The above band and bust measurements can be used in the size chart to find your size.
Inches: Add 5 inches to your band measurement and round down to the nearest even number, this gives you your band size. Subtract your band size from your bust measurement. The difference equals your cup size.

0" IN = A Cup
1" IN = B Cup
2" IN = C Cup
3" IN = D Cup

Example Bra Size Calculation (Inches):

  • 31.5”(Band Measurement) + 5” = 36.5”. Round down to 36 (Band Size)
  • Bust at fullest is 37.5”, round up to 38”(Bust Measurement).
  • 38”(Bust Measurement) - 36”(Band Size) = 2” = C Cup Size
  • Bra Size = 36C

3. WAIST

Measure around the smallest part of your natural waistline where your body bends side to side.

4. HIP

Measure at the fullest part of your low hip. Tape should fit comfortably.

5. FOOT LENGTH

Measure from the back of your heel to the tip of your longest toe. Measure both feet and choose the longer one if they are different lengths.
